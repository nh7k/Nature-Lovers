Plant Care Tips

Light Requirements

Most plants need 6-8 hours of sunlight daily. Indoor plants prefer bright, indirect light.

Watering

Water when the top inch of soil feels dry. Avoid overwatering as it can harm roots.

Fertilizing

Feed plants monthly during growing season with appropriate fertilizer for best growth.
